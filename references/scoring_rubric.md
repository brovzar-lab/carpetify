# EFICINE Producción 2026 — Scoring Rubric

> Source: Lineamientos y Requisitos para la Evaluación de los Proyectos de EFICINE Producción, enero 2026, Capítulo IV, Sección I, Apartado A.

## Minimum to Pass: 90/100 points
## Average Winning Score (2025 Period 1): 94.63/100

---

## SCORING BREAKDOWN — PRODUCTION PROJECTS

### ARTISTIC, CULTURAL & CINEMATIC MERIT (up to 62 points)

| Category | Max Points | Document | What Evaluators Score |
|----------|-----------|----------|----------------------|
| **Guion o argumento** | **40** | A3 | Dramatic progression, coherence and logic of the story; clear and congruent character construction, situations, and/or dialogue; elements that sustain the plot and proposed structure; point of view, originality, and/or relevance of themes. For documentary: clarity of approach, relevance/originality of theme, depth of prior research, narrative structure fluidity. |
| **Propuesta creativa de dirección** | **12** | A4 | How the director plans to realize the film (creative + technical); language choices and coherence with the screenplay; how it reflects in actor/crew direction. For animation: technique description precision, work stages, collaboration strategy. For documentary: approach to participants and community. PLUS the director's trajectory (filmography, awards). |
| **Material visual y propuestas del personal creativo** | **10** | A5 | Pertinence, coherence, and quality of proposals from creative areas (DP, art direction, editing); visual/conceptual elements (participants, research, locations, wardrobe, makeup, SFX/VFX, casting proposal, reference images). For animation: character designs, backgrounds, storyboards. Plus up to 2 audiovisual hyperlinks. |

**Subtotal artistic: 62 points max**

### PRODUCTION & DISTRIBUTION VIABILITY (up to 38 points)

| Category | Max Points | Document | What Evaluators Score |
|----------|-----------|----------|----------------------|
| **Solidez y experiencia del equipo creativo** | **2** | A6 (FORMATO 2) | Professional trajectory in features/shorts/audiovisual; capacity to complete project in time, form, and quality; national/international circulation; coherence between experience and proposals/budget; prior collaborations demonstrating solid creative dynamic. Mixed teams (experienced + emerging) are acceptable. |
| **Propuesta de producción** | **12** | A7 | Producer's creative contribution; work plan solidity; timeline coherence per stage; crew organization per stage; identification of practical and budgetary challenges; commitment to respectful work environment. For international co-production: pertinence and solidity of Mexican participation. |
| **Plan de rodaje + Ruta crítica** | **10** | A8a + A8b | **Plan de rodaje:** clarity of day-by-day organization, feasibility of assigned time, correspondence between schedule and budget. **Ruta crítica:** precision in monthly detail across all stages; correspondence with direction/production proposals and budget; risk anticipation capacity; workflow continuity; integral planning aligned with project needs. |
| **Presupuesto (resumen + desglose + flujo de efectivo)** | **10** | A9a + A9b + A9d | Clarity, coherence, and viability of financial structure; pertinence and justification of resource allocation per line item; proportionality of crew fees to experience and project magnitude; clear distinction of cash vs. in-kind contributions; congruence with flujo and esquema financiero. For co-productions: national vs. foreign spend split with exchange rate. |
| **Propuesta de exhibición** | **4** | A10 | Distribution/commercialization strategy coherent with exhibition circuit; cultural/social circulation strategy; target audience definition (age, gender) coherent with the film; estimated copies/screens/projections and spectators/revenue coherent with the film AND its production cost; festival strategy (if applicable) with justified selection. |

**Subtotal viability: 38 points max**

## BONUS POINTS (+5, non-cumulative)

These 5 bonus points are added to the CE Producción score. Only ONE category can apply per project — they are NOT stackable.

| Category | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **(a) Female director** | Directed by a woman, NOT in co-direction with a man. |
| **(b) Indigenous/Afro-Mexican director** | Directed by a person who self-identifies as indigenous or Afro-Mexican community member (per Art. 2 of the Constitution), NOT in co-direction with a non-member. Requires carta de autoadscripción. |
| **(c) Regional decentralization** | Produced or directed by a person from outside ZMCM (Zona Metropolitana de la Ciudad de México), with ≥75% of filming outside ZMCM, ≥50% local creative team, ≥50% local technical crew, AND the ERPI's fiscal domicile is outside ZMCM. |
| **(d) 100% qualifying creative team** | Director is NOT female nor indigenous/Afro-Mexican, BUT 100% of the creative team (producer, screenwriter, DP, art director, editor) are women OR indigenous/Afro-Mexican. No co-direction with non-qualifying individuals. If not all 5 roles exist, post-production roles may substitute (post supervisor, sound design, music composition). |

## SCORING FOR POSTPRODUCTION PROJECTS (Different rubric)

If the project is submitted in the postproduction modality (filming already complete), the scoring changes:

| Category | Max Points | Document |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Material filmado (first cut) | **65** | Video link with EFICINE watermark |
| Solidez y experiencia del equipo creativo | **2** | FORMATO 2 |
| Escrito libre de postproducción | **15** | Max 5 cuartillas |
| Ruta crítica | **4** | Monthly detail for post stages |
| Presupuesto (resumen + desglose + flujo) | **10** | Full project budget including prior stages |
| Propuesta de exhibición | **4** | Same as production |

**Minimum to pass: 90/100**

## EVALUATION GROUP ASSIGNMENT

Projects are assigned to evaluation groups based on two criteria:

| Director Category | Budget Threshold | Group |
|-------------------|-----------------|-------|
| Ópera prima | < $20M MXN | Group A |
| Ópera prima | ≥ $20M MXN | Group B |
| 2nd feature + subsequent | < $25M MXN | Group C |
| 2nd feature + subsequent | ≥ $25M MXN | Group D |

Each group has 3-5 evaluators selected by lottery (insaculación) with gender equity, geographic representation, and genre expertise considerations.

---

## EVALUATION VALIDITY

- A favorable evaluation is valid for **two consecutive periods**: the period when issued AND the immediately following period.
- Exception: if the evaluation was issued in the second period of the fiscal year AND scored ≥95/100 points, the validity extends to the first period of the next fiscal year.

## SELF-SCORING ENGINE (App Feature)

The app should estimate the project's score before export. This is NOT a prediction of the committee's decision — it's a quality assurance tool.

### Scoring Signals to Flag (WARNING level):

| Signal | Risk |
|--------|------|
| Propuesta de dirección < 2 cuartillas | Likely scoring below 10/12 on dirección |
| No prior director filmography links provided | Likely scoring 0 on dirección trajectory |
| Plan de rodaje has >5 pages/day for drama | Schedule may be flagged as unrealistic |
| Budget has no contingency line | Evaluators will question viability |
| Propuesta de exhibición doesn't estimate spectators | Loses coherence points |
| No festival strategy for art-house genre | Missed opportunity for exhibición points |
| Propuesta de producción doesn't mention safe workplace | Evaluators explicitly check for this |
| Budget crew rates significantly below market | Evaluators question viability and team solidity |
| Ruta crítica doesn't show monthly detail | Loses points for precision |
| Material visual is <10 pages for a fiction film | May appear underdeveloped |
