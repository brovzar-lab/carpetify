# Family History Research

Provides assistance with planning family history and genealogy research projects.

## When to Use This Skill

- Provides assistance with planning family history and genealogy research projects.

## Instructions

1. Help organize known family information into a research plan
2. Suggest record sources (census, vital records, immigration)
3. Guide structured research methodology
4. Help interpret historical records and naming conventions
5. Track research progress and document findings
