# Meeting Insights Analyzer

Analyzes meeting transcripts to uncover behavioral patterns including conflict avoidance, speaking ratios, filler words, and leadership style.

## When to Use This Skill

- Analyzes meeting transcripts to uncover behavioral patterns including conflict avoidance, speaking ratios, filler words, and leadership style.

## Instructions

1. Parse meeting transcript and identify speakers
2. Calculate speaking time ratios per participant
3. Detect filler words, hedging language, and interruptions
4. Identify conflict avoidance patterns and decision-making styles
5. Generate insights report with actionable recommendations
