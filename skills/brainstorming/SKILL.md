# Brainstorming

Transform rough ideas into fully-formed designs through structured questioning and alternative exploration.

## When to Use This Skill

- Transform rough ideas into fully-formed designs through structured questioning and alternative exploration.

## Instructions

1. Ask clarifying questions to understand the core idea
2. Generate 3-5 alternative approaches or framings
3. Explore pros/cons and trade-offs of each
4. Help user converge on a refined design
5. Document the final design with rationale
