# Competitive Ads Extractor

Extracts and analyzes competitors' ads from ad libraries to understand messaging and creative approaches that resonate.

## When to Use This Skill

- Extracts and analyzes competitors' ads from ad libraries to understand messaging and creative approaches that resonate.

## Instructions

1. Accept competitor names or ad library URLs
2. Extract ad creatives, copy, CTAs, and targeting signals
3. Analyze messaging patterns, themes, and tone
4. Compare against your own messaging for gaps and opportunities
5. Generate competitive ad intelligence report
