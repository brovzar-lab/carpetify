# Tailored Resume Generator

Analyzes job descriptions and generates tailored resumes that highlight relevant experience, skills, and achievements to maximize interview chances.

## When to Use This Skill

- Analyzes job descriptions and generates tailored resumes that highlight relevant experience, skills, and achievements to maximize interview chances.

## Instructions

1. Parse job description for key requirements and keywords
2. Match candidate experience and skills to requirements
3. Rewrite bullet points to emphasize relevant achievements
4. Optimize for ATS keyword matching
5. Generate formatted resume in requested format (PDF, DOCX, Markdown)
