# Invoice Organizer

Automatically organizes invoices and receipts for tax preparation by reading files, extracting information, and renaming consistently.

## When to Use This Skill

- Automatically organizes invoices and receipts for tax preparation by reading files, extracting information, and renaming consistently.

## Instructions

1. Scan directory for invoice/receipt files (PDF, image, etc.)
2. Extract: vendor, date, amount, category from each
3. Rename files with consistent format: YYYY-MM-DD_Vendor_Amount
4. Organize into folders by category or tax quarter
5. Generate summary spreadsheet of all invoices
