# Lead Research Assistant

Identifies and qualifies high-quality leads by analyzing your product, searching for target companies, and providing actionable outreach strategies.

## When to Use This Skill

- Identifies and qualifies high-quality leads by analyzing your product, searching for target companies, and providing actionable outreach strategies.

## Instructions

1. Analyze product/service value proposition and ICP
2. Search for companies matching ideal customer profile
3. Research company signals (funding, hiring, tech stack, pain points)
4. Score and rank leads by qualification criteria
5. Generate personalized outreach recommendations for top leads
